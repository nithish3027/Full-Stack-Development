Fox

Foxes are small-to-medium-sized omnivorous mammals belonging to the family Canidae, characterized by a flattened skull, upright triangular ears, a pointed snout, and a long, bushy tail. They are highly adaptable survivors found on every continent except Antarctica, thriving in diverse habitats ranging from Arctic tundras and deserts to urban environments.
